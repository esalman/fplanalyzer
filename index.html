FPLAnalyzer

This is a bookmarklet that allows you to preview opponents on different GW (and do other stuff in future, hopefully) on fantasy.premierleague.com.

Screenshot

Motivation

I have been playing FPL since 2007/08. Last couple of years I started to rely more and more on statistics. When picking team or transferring, there are a handful of stats that I need to check frequently. I created this bookmarklet to aid in that process. I only plan to add features to this bookmarklet which I need for myself. But I've open-sourced it so that anyone else can modify it and make their own version.

Also I think the FPL website is not very modern or dynamic although they launched a redesign at the beginning of 2012/13 season. So I thought I'd use some of my programming skills to add some useful interactivity to it.

How To Setup

  • Create a new bookmark on your browser.
  • Name of the bookmark: anything

URL of the bookmark:

  • Save the boomark (preferably on browser bookmark bar)

How To Use

  • Go to your 'Pick Team' or Transfer page on FPL site.
  • Click on the bookmark (first time click loads script on background)
  • Navigate to any gameweek by clicking on the link in the fixture list below your team.
  • Now click the bookmark. The opponent names below each player should change accordingly.

You can use it for strategizing in different ways. On Pick Team page, navigate to different gameweeks and click the bookmark to see if your current team is any good for the forseeable future. On Transfer page, shuffle your team, navigate to another GW and click the bookmark to strategize.
